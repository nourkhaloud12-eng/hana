Place your audio files in this folder:

- `birthday.mp3` — background music to play on page open (looped). Use a short, gentle birthday or party instrumental. If you want a specific song, copy it here and name it `birthday.mp3`.
- `puff.mp3` — short puff sound to play when the candle is blown. Optional.

Notes:
- If audio does not autoplay due to browser policies, click the speaker icon in the header to enable playback.
- Use royalty-free or permission-granted audio.

Suggestions (royalty-free sources):
- https://freesound.org
- https://www.free-stock-music.com/

After adding the files the app will play `birthday.mp3` automatically when the curtain opens and when you toggle the audio control.
